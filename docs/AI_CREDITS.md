# SnapNext AI Credits

AI Credits are user-facing capacity units, not additional charges.

Weekly allowances:
- Free: 20
- Plus: 80
- Pro: 180
- Family: 280 shared

Rules:
- Cached AI results use 0 credits.
- Uploads, gallery, memories, downloads, and local organization continue after credits are exhausted.
- Optional paid-provider AI pauses until the weekly reset or plan upgrade.
- Credits reset every Monday UTC.
- One credit represents $0.001 of maximum reserved external AI capacity internally; users never see or pay this conversion directly.
